Description

  • 94% Merlot,
  • 3% Cabernet Sauvignon,

Grilled steaks, roasted pork, chicken, and red meat.

Full with destiny and bursting with flavor. Dark fruit, cherry, blackberry, brimming with cedar, pipe tobacco, and earth.

Sundance, Art Den Hoed, Goose Ridge, Weyns.
